Mountaineering Mt. McKinley

Mountaineering Mt. McKinley Highlights of the mountaineering cycling tour includes: a two-day mountaineering skills refresher course, breathtaking views of the majestic Alaskan wilderness, a victory feast of fresh Alaskan King Crab or Salmon, and a guided tour of Fairbanks.


White-Water Rafting The Colorado River

White-Water Rafting the Colorado River Highlightsof the river tours include: ahike from the South Rim down to the Colorado River, traversing more than 27 rapid fields, an overnight stay at Phantom Ranch, hikes to Elves Chasm and Keyhole Arch; and a mule-train ride back up to the South Rim.

Scuba Diving

Scuba Diving the Four Corners Highlights of this 17-night, fully escorted, scuba diving tour include: three nights in Sydney, an excursion to Ayers Rock, two nights aboard the luxurious divning-platform ship Nemo, and oceanography lectures sponsored by the University of New South Wales.
